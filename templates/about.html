  • Covid Info

    • This COVID-19 scanner works by taking an x-ray scan image as the input. When an input image is recieved, the image is preprocessed and then passed through a convolutional neural network. The network then predicts the probability of the individual having covid.

    After an image is uploaded and the submit button is clicked, the website displays the final verdict of the model. A 'Covid: Positive' output would suggest the individual in the x-ray scan had covid at the time of the scan. A negative result would suggest the individual was free of covid at the time.

    Below the final verdict you will find the exact probabilities for both possibilities (Covid or Normal). You will also find your image at the bottom of the page.

    This covid scanner works best with square (equal length and width) png images.
